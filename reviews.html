These are not casual rankings. Reviews here ask whether a healthcare AI tool or AI infrastructure claim survives contact with workflow, data, integration, and review reality.

Review framework

  1. What workflow does it claim to improve?
  2. What evidence would make the claim credible?
  3. What integration, data, or review burden remains?
  4. Who should use it?
  5. Who should be skeptical?
