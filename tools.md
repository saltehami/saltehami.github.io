This page is for my own tools, checklists, workflows, and small artifacts. External healthcare AI products and infrastructure belong in [Reviews](/reviews/), where the question is whether a claim should be trusted.

## Writing

- A static site, because fast pages and durable URLs matter.
- Notes that can turn into essays without needing a publishing ceremony.
- Dates, source links, and caveats when the claim depends on evidence.

## Data work

- SQL when the question needs a spine.
- Spreadsheets when the workflow is still being discovered.
- Small scripts when the repeated mistake is obvious.

## AI buildout

- Agents when they are bounded by a real task and a review path.
- Retrieval when the source boundary matters.
- Evals when the failure mode is too expensive to discover by vibes.

## Operations

- Checklists for recurring work.
- Reports that show expected, observed, and unexplained states.
- Dashboards only when the metric can be traced back to a real source.
